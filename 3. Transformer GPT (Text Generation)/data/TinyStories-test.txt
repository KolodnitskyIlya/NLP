Once upon a time, there was a little rabbit named Ben. Ben lived in a small garden with many green leaves. He liked to jump and run in the sun.

One day, Ben saw a big red apple under a tree. He wanted to eat it, but the apple was too high on a small box. Ben tried to reach it, but he was too small.

Then, Ben saw his friend, a tall goat named Lily. Lily helped Ben get the apple. Ben said, "Thank you, Lily!" They shared the apple and played in the garden all day.

Ben learned that friends can help when something is hard.
